Common Questions

VA Training FAQs

Is ABI really VA-approved?

Yes. ABI's programs are approved for veterans' training in accordance with Title 38 United States Code § 3676. Eligible veterans present a certificate of eligibility and discharge papers (Form DD-214) to use their benefits.

Which VA benefits can I use?

ABI accepts the Post-9/11 GI Bill® (Ch. 33), VR&E (Ch. 31), the Montgomery GI Bill® and DEA (Ch. 35). Details are on our veterans benefits page.

I have a service-connected disability — is there extra support?

Yes. VR&E (Ch. 31) exists specifically for veterans with service-connected disabilities, and ABI provides accommodations and an inclusive learning environment. New Yorkers with documented disabilities may also explore ACCES-VR.
