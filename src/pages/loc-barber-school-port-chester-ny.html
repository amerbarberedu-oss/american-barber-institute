About Our Program

A Westchester Barber School Within Commuting Reach

Our students embark on a four-month journey that begins with two weeks of intensive barber theory — sanitation, sterilization, barber history, barber laws and shop management — then shifts to serious hands-on training with experienced instructors.

Quick Answers

Questions Port Chester Students Ask

How much of the training is hands-on?

The majority. After two weeks of barber theory, our instructors devote considerable time to hands-on work — fades, shaving, facial massage and hot towel technique.

How much does it cost?

Payment plans start from $150 per week for the 500-Hour Master Barber program, which most students finish in about 4 months.

Will ABI help me find a job?

Yes — at program completion every student can engage in job placement discussions, and our placement office connects graduates with shops and salons that are actively hiring.
