Quick Answers

Questions Brooklyn Students Ask

How long does the program take?

The 500-Hour Master Barber program takes about 4 months to complete, and new classes begin on the first Monday of each month.

Can I afford it?

Yes — payment plans start from $150 per week, and our admissions team can walk you through financial assistance options like ACCES-VR and VA education benefits.

Is ABI a licensed school?

ABI has been licensed by the New York State Education Department since 1996, with curriculum and instructors reviewed and approved by the State.
