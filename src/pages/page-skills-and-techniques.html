Skills & Techniques

Everything our students master over four months — from barber theory and sanitation to fades, razor work and State Board exam prep.

The Curriculum

What You'll Learn at ABI

Throughout our comprehensive four-month program, students delve into the intricacies of barbering through the study of barber theory and daily hands-on practice with a diverse, real clientele.

1

Barber Theory & Sanitation

The foundation of a professional career: maintaining impeccable sanitation and sterilization standards, understanding the rich history of the barbering profession, adhering to relevant barbering laws, and mastering the habits of a successful barber career.

  • Sanitation & sterilization standards
  • Barbering history & NY barbering law
2

Cutting & Fading

From the timeless elegance of classic tapers and pompadours to the bold statements of fohawks and baldies, students become adept at crafting a wide array of hairstyles on every hair type.

  • Scissors cuts, clipper cuts & caesars
  • Low, mid, high & high-top fades
  • Afro cuts, flat tops & razor lineups
3

Shaving, Styling & Care

Beyond the cut, students acquire expertise in the full range of traditional barbering services that keep clients coming back to the chair.

  • The art of shaving & razor work
  • Therapeutic facial massage
  • Shampoos, blowouts & styling techniques
