Why ABI Graduates

What You Get When You Hire From ABI

Our graduates arrive with 500 hours of NYS-licensed training behind them — not just theory, but daily work on real clients.

Board-Prepared, Shop-Ready

Every student completes the 500-Hour Master Barber Program — fades, tapers, shears, razor work, shaves and sanitation — and is prepared for the New York State Board Exam. They've cut hair on a diverse, live clientele from day one.

Professionalism Included

During training we prepare students for the real needs of an employer: interviewing, etiquette, and the professional boundaries between barber, employer and clientele. You get a hire who understands how a shop actually runs.

A Pipeline, Not a One-Off

New classes graduate throughout the year from both our Manhattan and Bronx campuses, so registering your shop once keeps you connected to a steady stream of fresh, licensed-track talent — established in 1996, ABI has been supplying New York shops with barbers for nearly three decades.

Part-Time Help Too

Our placement office also serves current students looking to work part-time while still in school — a practical way to trial a future hire in your shop before they even graduate.
