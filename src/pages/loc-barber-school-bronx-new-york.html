Quick Answers

Questions Bronx Students Ask

Where exactly is the campus?

121 Westchester Square, Bronx, NY 10461 — in the heart of the Westchester Square neighborhood. Call (718) 676-0640 for directions or to book a tour.

How long until I can work?

The 500-Hour Master Barber program takes about 4 months, and it prepares you for the New York State licensing exam — written and practical.

Can I see the school before enrolling?

Absolutely — book a tour, watch a class in action, and grab a $3 student haircut while you're here.
