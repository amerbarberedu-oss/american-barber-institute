Quick Answers

Before You Stop By

Why are haircuts only $3?

Because every cut is part of a lesson. Supervised students need real clients to master their craft, so the community gets quality cuts at a fraction of barbershop prices.

Are the cuts any good?

High in quality, low in price — that's been the promise since 1996. Licensed instructors supervise every chair, checking the work before you leave.

What styles can I ask for?

We specialize in scissor-over-comb, classical cuts, fades, Mohawks, caesars, shape-ups, baldys and hot leather shaves — plus children's haircuts.
