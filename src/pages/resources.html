What We Offer

Help With Cost, Benefits & Licensing

Four ways ABI helps you fund your education and get licensed to work in New York.

1

ACCES-VR Financial Assistance

A New York State program for individuals with documented disabilities. If you qualify, it can cover tuition, tools and supplies — at no cost to you.

  • Tuition, tools & supplies potentially covered
  • ABI assists with the referral process
Talk to admissions →
2

Veterans & GI Bill®

ABI accepts the Post-9/11 GI Bill® (Ch. 33), VR&E (Ch. 31), Montgomery GI Bill® and DEA (Ch. 35) and certifies your enrollment with the VA.

  • Benefits can cover all or part of tuition
  • We handle VA enrollment certification
VA education benefits →
3

Licensing — NY State Board

Every student is prepared for the New York State Board of Barbering Examination — written and practical — required to work in any NY barbershop.

  • Full written + practical exam prep
  • Earn your Master Barber License
View the 500-Hour program →
4

Tools & Supplies

Students purchase their own tools, books and supplies — directly from ABI at registration or from approved third-party suppliers.

  • Detailed tools list provided at registration
  • Buy from ABI or approved suppliers
