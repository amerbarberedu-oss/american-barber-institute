Privacy Policy

How American Barber Institute collects, uses and protects your personal information.

Our Commitment to You

At American Barber Institute, we prioritize the protection and security of your personal information. We highly value our relationship with you and work diligently to maintain your privacy while honoring your preferences. As part of our relationship, there may be instances where we need to collect or share certain information about you. This privacy policy statement outlines how we handle customer information.

Information We Collect

We collect the information you provide to us through applications, surveys, registration forms, and other similar channels. This may include your name, phone number, email address, mailing address and any details you choose to share when you request information, book a tour, register for a program, or register a barbershop with our job placement office.

Forms, Chat & Website Analytics

Our website uses forms and chat widgets provided by LeadConnector (GoHighLevel) to let you request information, book tours and contact our team. Information you submit through these forms and chat conversations is processed by LeadConnector on our behalf so that we can respond to your inquiry.

We also use third-party analytics and advertising cookies to understand how visitors use our site and to measure the effectiveness of our advertising. These services may set cookies or similar technologies in your browser:

  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage statistics such as pages visited and time on site.
  • Meta Pixel (Facebook) — advertising measurement and audience building.
  • Microsoft Clarity — session analytics that help us improve the usability of the site.

You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Doing so will not prevent you from browsing the site, though some features (such as forms or chat) may not work as intended.

How We Share Information

We do not disclose any information we possess about you to any third party unless required by law or in response to legal requests.

In certain circumstances, the law permits us to share information about our current and former customers with government agencies or authorized third parties. This disclosure may be necessary in response to subpoenas or to comply with specific legal obligations.

How We Protect Information

We prioritize the security of your data and take precautions to safeguard it from unauthorized access.

Former Customers

We do not share any information regarding our former customers with any external party unless compelled to do so by law or legal obligations.

Changes to This Policy

Please note that this policy is subject to change, and any updates will be communicated to you promptly. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our policy or privacy practices, please feel free to contact us.
