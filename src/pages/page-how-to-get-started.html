Step by Step

Four Steps to Enrollment

American Barber Institute is licensed by the New York State Department of Education, and our Admissions Office is happy to answer any question along the way. If your circumstances are unusual, tell us — we'll work with you to remove obstacles to enrolling.

1

Reach Out & Book a Tour

Call us or book a free campus tour in Manhattan or the Bronx. You'll meet our staff, see the training floors and get every question answered on the spot.

  • English: (212) 290-2289 · Español: (212) 290-0278
  • Bronx campus: (718) 676-0640
Book your tour →
2

Gather Your Documents

Bring these to registration. No High School Diploma or GED? ABI administers an entrance exam before enrolling. Foreign diplomas need a notarized translation plus a copy of the original.

  • Social Security card & photo ID or driver's license
  • HSD or GED (or pass ABI's entrance exam)
  • Proof of address · at least 17 years of age
  • Down payment
Questions about requirements? →
3

Pick Your Schedule & Plan

Choose morning (Mon–Fri 8 AM–2 PM), afternoon (Mon–Fri 2 PM–8 PM) or weekend (Sat–Sun 9 AM–7 PM). Every schedule includes a weekly payment plan — from $150 per week — so you pay while you train.

  • Full-time students finish in as little as 4 months
  • Tools, books & supplies purchased separately
Compare programs & tuition →
4

Start on the First Monday

New classes begin the first Monday of each month. Register, get your tools list, and start your 500-Hour Master Barber training with hands-on practice from day one.

  • Classes start the first Monday of every month
  • Reserve your seat with admissions in advance

Before You Enroll

Getting-Started FAQs

What if I don't have a High School Diploma or GED?

You can still enroll — ABI administers an entrance exam prior to enrollment. If your diploma is from a foreign country, submit a notarized translation along with a copy of the original document.

How old do I have to be?

You must be at least 17 years of age to enroll in the program.

When can I start?

New classes begin on the first Monday of every month at both campuses. Call (212) 290-2289 to reserve your seat for the next start date.
