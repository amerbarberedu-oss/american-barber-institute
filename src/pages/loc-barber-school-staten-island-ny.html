Quick Answers

Questions Staten Island Students Ask

How long is the program?

The 500-Hour Master Barber program runs about 4 months, and new classes begin on the first Monday of each month.

What will it cost me?

Payment plans start from $150 per week — and admissions can walk you through ACCES-VR financial assistance and VA education benefits if you qualify.

Can I open my own shop one day?

That's the goal for many of our students. ABI's training and NYS Master Barber license prep are designed so graduates can work anywhere — or open a barbershop of their own.
