Quick Answers

Questions Westchester Students Ask

Do students really commute from Westchester?

Yes — students have traveled to ABI from across Westchester County for years, and our Bronx campus at Westchester Square makes the trip shorter than ever.

What's covered in the training?

Sanitation and sterilization, barber history, NY State board exam prep, plus hands-on facial massage, hot towel shaves, styling, haircuts and shaving.

What happens after graduation?

Graduates are offered the opportunity to meet with potential employers, and our job placement office keeps working with you until you land a chair.
