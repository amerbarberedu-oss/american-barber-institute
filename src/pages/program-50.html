Common Questions

The Refresher Route, Answered

Do I still have to take the state exam after the refresher?
Yes. The refresher gets you exam-ready; you then sit for the New York State Board barber exam. Passing it earns your Master Barber license. Our exam guide covers what to expect.
I'm a complete beginner — can I take the 50-hour course?
No. The refresher assumes existing licensure or apprentice experience. Beginners need the 500-hour program, which is built from the ground up.
Is the refresher available at every location?
It's offered at the Manhattan campus only (48 West 39th Street). The full 500-hour route is available more widely, including our Bronx campus.
How is the $1,500 paid?
In two installments: $750 at registration and $750 at the start of week two. The total includes the $100 registration fee. Tools and supplies are purchased separately before classes start.
How long does the refresher take?
About two weeks — 50 hours delivered as 10 five-hour days, five days a week. New cohorts start the first Monday of each month.
