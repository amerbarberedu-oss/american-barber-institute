About the Program

Get Hands-On & Pass the Exam

The 50-Hour Barber Refresher program is intended primarily for licensed individuals wishing to enhance their skills — Cosmetologists, Hairdressers, and Barber Apprentices ready to take the NY State Board Exam to become a Master Barber.

Please note: the Barber Refresher course only applies for our Manhattan location (48 West 39th Street, New York, NY 10018) — it is not offered at the Bronx campus.

At ABI, we make sure every student is up to speed on classical haircutting, straight razor shaves, fades, tapers, scissor-over-comb techniques and modern hairstyles — properly prepared to sit for and pass the New York State Barbering Licensing Examination. We provide students with real clients for plenty of hands-on training.

Upon completion, students receive a 50-Hour Barber Refresher certificate, and we provide job placement assistance to every graduate.
