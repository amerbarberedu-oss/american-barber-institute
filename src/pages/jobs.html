For Barbers

Job Placement Office

Every ABI student gets a one-on-one meeting with our placement office — a comprehensive review of the employment prospects waiting for them, tailored to individual goals and skills. We maintain a full-time office for graduates and for students who want to work part-time while still in school.

  • Four Months TrainingGraduate in as little as 4 months — a career-oriented, essentially recession-proof skill.
  • High Income PotentialMany barbers are their own boss and get paid every day. You decide how hard you work.
  • Be Your Own BossFew careers offer this freedom: control your own income and schedule.
  • Strong Job AvailabilityDemand for licensed barbers is strong — many shops need help right now.

We can't guarantee placement — practical skill, conduct, and professionalism all matter. During training we prepare you for the real needs of an employer: interviewing, etiquette, and the boundaries between you, your employer, and clientele.
