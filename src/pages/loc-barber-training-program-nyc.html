Quick Answers

About Our NYC Training Program

What's the end goal of the program?

Passing the New York State Board of Barbering license test and becoming a Master Barber — everything in the curriculum builds toward that.

How long does training take?

The 500-Hour Master Barber program takes about 4 months, with payment plans starting from $150 per week.

Do you teach the business side?

Yes — professionalism, customer service and business management are part of the curriculum, so graduates can succeed as barbers and as entrepreneurs.
