About the checklist

Is the checklist really free?

Yes. It's completely free — we send it to your inbox so you have it saved and printable whenever you need it. No payment, no obligation to enroll anywhere.

Do I have to enroll at ABI to use it?

Not at all. The checklist reflects New York's general licensing steps and is useful no matter where you train. If you decide ABI is your school, we're here — but the guide stands on its own.

Where do the requirements come from?

The steps follow the licensing process published by the New York Department of State, Division of Licensing Services. Always verify current fees and forms on the official state site before you file.

How current is it?

It's maintained for 2026. Because state requirements can change, the checklist links you back to the official New York Department of State resources for the latest details.
