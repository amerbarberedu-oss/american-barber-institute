Quick Answers

Questions Long Island Students Ask

Is the commute really doable?

Many of our students do it every day — the LIRR drops you at Penn Station, and our Manhattan campus at 48 West 39th Street is just minutes away on foot.

How fast can I finish?

The 500-Hour Master Barber program takes about 4 months, with new classes beginning on the first Monday of each month.

Will I find work back home?

When you graduate, our job placement office helps you become gainfully employed anywhere in the New York area — including Long Island.
