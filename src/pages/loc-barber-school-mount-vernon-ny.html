About Our Program

Westchester Barber Courses, Minutes from Mount Vernon

Over four months, students gain the skills and knowledge essential to a successful barbering career. The program opens with two weeks of intensive barber theory — sanitation, sterilization, barber history, barber laws and shop management — before moving to the training floor.

Quick Answers

Questions Mount Vernon Students Ask

How is the program structured?

Four months total: it opens with two weeks of intensive barber theory — sanitation, sterilization, history, laws and shop management — then moves into hands-on training.

Which campus is closest?

The Bronx campus at 121 Westchester Square is just over the county line from Mount Vernon. Call (718) 676-0640 to visit.

Can barbering really support me?

Many barbers run their own businesses and earn daily income — some out-earning certain college graduates. Your effort sets your ceiling.
