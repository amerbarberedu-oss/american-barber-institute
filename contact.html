Directions

Subway: The B, D, F, M, N, Q, R, W, S, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 and 7 trains all run within a few short blocks of the school (Bryant Park / Times Square / Herald Square stations).

Bus & rail: The M5, M7, M20, M42 and M104 stop within a block or two. The Port Authority Bus Terminal — with frequent service to New Jersey, Westchester, Upstate NY, Long Island and Queens — and Metro-North at Grand Central are minutes away.
