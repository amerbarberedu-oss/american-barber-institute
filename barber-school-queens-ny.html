Why Queens Students Choose ABI

ABI has been featured in magazines, online publications, the NY Post and on TV networks for excellence in barber education. We've built our reputation on quality training since 1996.

Our Manhattan campus at 48 West 39th Street sits minutes from Penn Station, Grand Central and Times Square — roughly a 30-minute trip from Queens on most subway lines into Midtown.

Instructors Who Love to Teach

Barber Programs for Queens Residents

Every program is professionally evaluated and approved — ABI is licensed by the New York State Education Department.

Flagship

500-Hour Master Barber

Finish in about 4 months and graduate ready for the New York State board exam. Payment plans from $150/week make it affordable.

Sharpen your skills and update your technique. Offered at our Manhattan campus only — ideal for licensed barbers returning to the chair.
