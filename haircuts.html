Why Just $3?

Real Cuts, Real Training

Every cut at ABI is performed by a student barber working toward the 500 hours required for graduation — under the direct supervision of our licensed master instructors at all times. You get a great cut at an incredible value, and our students get the hands-on, real-client practice that makes them job-ready.

Please note: Haircuts are performed by students under the supervision of licensed instructors. Student barbers are not yet licensed professionals.

$3per haircut · additional services available at an extra cost

How It Works

Three Simple Steps

01

Call to Schedule

Contact us at (212) 290-2289 to book your haircut appointment. Walk-ins are also welcome based on availability.

02

Come In

Visit our Manhattan or Bronx location during business hours. Our student barbers are supervised by experienced instructors at all times.

03

Get Your Cut

Consult with your student barber, describe your desired style, and let them work. Classic cuts, fades, trims — all at an incredible value.
